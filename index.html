OUR APPROACH

Built on Discipline, Not Guesswork

Alpha Engine doesn't chase every candle. Every decision the system makes — when to enter, when to wait, when to protect profit — comes from the same three principles.

Multi-Timeframe Confirmation

No single candle triggers a trade. Multiple timeframes — from 15 minutes up to Daily — must agree before the system acts.

One Trade at a Time, No Pyramiding

The system never stacks positions on top of an open trade. Every entry starts from flat — clean, countable, accountable.

Risk Is Managed, Not Hoped For

Every trade carries a defined stop and a structured exit plan from the moment it opens — profit-taking and protection are built in, not improvised.

THE SYSTEM

Three Mechanisms. One Engine.

Every signal Alpha Engine produces passes through three connected layers before it ever reaches your chart.

STEP 01

Entry Logic

Alpha Engine waits for agreement across multiple timeframes before marking an entry. A single 15-minute candle never triggers a trade — the signal only fires once the higher timeframes confirm the same directional bias.

STEP 02

Reversal System

When price structure breaks down, Alpha Engine doesn't hold onto a losing thesis. The reversal system watches for exhaustion and momentum shifts, flipping bias the moment a trend loses conviction.

STEP 03

Targets & Labels

Every trade is labeled the moment it opens — entry context, confidence tier, and a structured target ladder. Nothing is improvised after the fact; the exit plan exists before the position does.
